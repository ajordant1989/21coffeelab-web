21. Coffee Lab is a precision-roasted specialty coffee brand built on a simple idea: every variable matters. Just as Bitcoin's supply is fixed at 21 million, we believe great coffee has no shortcuts — only process, precision, and relentless attention to what's in the bag.

We source single-origin green beans, roast in small batches on a Bellwether countertop roaster, and deliver a product that's as honest as the blockchain it's named after. No blends that hide flaws. No vague origin stories. Just traceable, repeatable, exceptional coffee.

Based in Alabama. Shipping nationwide. Launching soon.
